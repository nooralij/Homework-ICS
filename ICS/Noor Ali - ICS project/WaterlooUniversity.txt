
	University of Waterloo:


	• Admission requirements include finishing Advanced Functions, Calculus and Vectors, Chemistry, 
	Physics, English, high school courses each with a minimal 70% final average

	• Students will learn engineering sciences in the Department of Electrical Engineering, and take 
	classes in Waterloo’s Cheriton School of Computer Science (one of the best in the world)

	• Students earn a Bachelor of Applied Science degree

	• It takes students 4 2/3 years to complete the degree requirements

	• The cost of this program is $14,557
